Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and features used.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs based on aggregated usage patterns.

We will never sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or alteration.

Your expense data is associated with your account and is not accessible to other users. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

We use Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface. This request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No personal or expense data is included in this request.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out to us through the contact information provided on the site.

We will respond to all privacy-related requests within a reasonable timeframe.
